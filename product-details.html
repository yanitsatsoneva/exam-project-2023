BM 2000 CP D

2-COMPONENT DOT APPLICATION (98:2)

From city areas to urban and interurban roads, the BM 2000 can handle both smaller jobs and longer road stretches.The BM 2000 CP D is an agile machine with excellent loading abilities and a turning radius of only 3,2 m. It allows for paint tank capacities of 440 L or 2 x 220 L.The BM 2000 has a one-seat slidable drive and operator section for flexible working on the left or right side.The Borum 2-component application equipment can be set to execute lines in widths between 10-30 cm. The equipment on this machine may be used for the application of profiled dots.
